Privacy Policy — The Morning Club

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1 Introduction

Welcome to The Morning Club ("the App", "we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

The Morning Club is a financial information application of a purely informational nature. The App provides news, briefings, market metrics, and financial educational content. It does not allow you to make investment transactions, buy, or sell financial assets.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2 Minimum Age

The use of The Morning Club is restricted to individuals aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16 years of age. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will promptly delete such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take the necessary steps.

3 Data We Collect

3.1 Registration and Authentication Data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — required for registration, login, and account recovery.
  • Password — stored securely using encryption. We never store it in plain text and have no access to it.
  • Username — chosen by you to identify yourself in the App's community.

3.2 Profile Data

  • Avatar — automatically generated from your username using an external avatar generation service (DiceBear). No personal photographs are uploaded.

3.3 Device Data (only through the feedback form)

When you voluntarily submit a bug report or suggestion through the App's feedback form, we collect the following technical device information solely for the purpose of diagnosing issues:

  • Device platform (iOS, Android, or Web).
  • Operating system version (e.g., "Android 14", "iOS 17.2").
  • Device model (e.g., "Pixel 8", "iPhone 15").
  • App version installed on your device.
Important: This device information is only collected when the user voluntarily submits a feedback report. We do not collect this information automatically or in the background.

3.4 Push Notification Token

If you agree to receive push notifications, we store your notification token to send you relevant alerts about news and market updates. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

3.5 User-Generated Content

  • Discussion threads and comments you post in the community section.
  • Feedback reports (title, description, feedback type) that you voluntarily submit.
  • Educational course progress (which lessons you have completed and your overall progress).

3.6 Subscription and Purchase Data

If you subscribe to a premium plan, we use RevenueCat as our subscription management platform. Through this service, the following data is collected and processed:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial, cancelled).
  • Transaction identifiers (purchase receipts and transaction IDs provided by the app store).
  • Subscription plan selected (type, tier, and billing period).
  • Purchase dates and renewal information.
Important: RevenueCat does not receive or process your credit card number, bank account details, or any direct payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play) through their respective payment systems. We have no access to your payment method details.
Note: By default, RevenueCat does not collect personally identifiable information such as names or email addresses. However, we may associate your anonymous app user ID with your account to manage your subscription entitlements.

3.7 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect geographic location data.
  • We do not collect personal financial data (bank accounts, cards, investment portfolios).
  • We do not collect biometric data.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising tracking technologies.
  • We do not perform automatic activity tracking or usage analytics.
  • We do not have access to your credit card or payment method details.

5 Data Storage and Security

All App data is stored on Supabase, a secure backend platform that uses:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications.
  • Encryption at rest for stored data.
  • Row Level Security (RLS) on the database, ensuring that each user can only access their own data.
  • Secure authentication tokens with automatic renewal.

Passwords are managed through Supabase's authentication system and are never stored in plain text.

7 Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your stored personal data.
  • Rectify your username and profile data directly from the App.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us directly.
  • Withdraw your consent for push notifications at any time from your device settings.
  • Object to the processing of your data in cases provided by applicable law.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format, upon request.

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this policy.

8 Data Retention

  • Account data: retained as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within a maximum of 30 days.
  • Subscription data: purchase history and transaction records may be retained as required by tax and legal obligations, even after account deletion. RevenueCat retains data as outlined in their privacy policy.
  • Feedback reports: retained for product improvement purposes. Associated device data is anonymized after 12 months.
  • Community content: published threads and comments may remain visible even after account deletion but will be disassociated from your identity.

9 Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make significant changes:

  • We will publish the updated version in the App.
  • We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this document.
  • In case of substantial changes, we will notify you through the App.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

10 International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers, including RevenueCat, are based in the United States. This means your subscription-related data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) that may have different data protection standards.

When such transfers occur, appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection.

11 Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by applicable data protection legislation, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union where applicable.
